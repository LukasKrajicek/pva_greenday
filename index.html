Green Day is an American rock band formed in Rodeo, California, in 1987 by lead vocalist and guitarist Billie Joe Armstrong and bassist and backing vocalist Mike Dirnt, with drummer Tré Cool joining in 1990.

In 1987, friends and guitarists Billie Joe Armstrong and Mike Dirnt, 15 years old at the time, along with bassist Sean Hughes and drummer Raj Punjabi, a fellow student from Pinole Valley High School, formed band "Blood Rage", the name taken from the eponymous film, and played together in Punjabi's father's house garage at Rodeo, California;

Green Day discography is 39/Smooth (1990) Kerplunk (1991) Dookie (1994) Insomniac (1995) Nimrod (1997) Warning (2000) American Idiot (2004) 21st Century Breakdown (2009) ¡Uno! (2012) ¡Dos! (2012) ¡Tré! (2012) Revolution Radio (2016) Father of All Motherfuckers (2020) Saviors (2024)
